Chocolate Chip Cookies (h2)

Millie Walsh Software Development Instructor Prime Digital Academy. Part time baker

These really are the best cookies ever. Thanks for giving them a try!

Ingredient List (Unordered List)

  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vinilla
  • 1 egg
  • etc.

Recipe (Ordered List)

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Mix wet ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Stir the dry ingredients in to the wet mixed ingredients.
  4. Place spoonfuls of dough onto a cookie sheet
  5. Bake for 8 - 10 minutes
